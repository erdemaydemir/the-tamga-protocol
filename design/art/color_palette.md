# Color Palette - The Tamga Protocol

**Version**: 1.0
**Last Updated**: 2026-01-31
**Art Style**: Retro Pixel Art (1990s Aesthetic)

---

## Overview

The Tamga Protocol uses a **16-color retro palette** inspired by 1990s Turkish aesthetics ‚Äî warm earth tones, muted colors, and a nostalgic CRT monitor feel.

**Design Philosophy**:
- Warm, earthy tones evoke 1990s Turkey
- Muted colors create retro atmosphere
- Limited palette enforces pixel art discipline
- No neon or overly saturated colors

---

## The 16-Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Browns & Earth Tones)

#### #8B4513 - Saddle Brown
**Usage**: Primary UI elements, backgrounds, borders
**RGB**: (139, 69, 19)
**Description**: Rich, warm brown. Main brand color.

#### #D2691E - Chocolate
**Usage**: Secondary UI elements, character clothing
**RGB**: (210, 105, 30)
**Description**: Medium warm brown for depth.

#### #F4A460 - Sandy Brown
**Usage**: Highlights, light sources, warm accents
**RGB**: (244, 164, 96)
**Description**: Light warm tone for highlights.

### Secondary Colors (Grays & Shadows)

#### #2F4F4F - Dark Slate Gray
**Usage**: Shadows, outlines, dark UI
**RGB**: (47, 79, 79)
**Description**: Deep shadow color, almost black.

#### #696969 - Dim Gray
**Usage**: Mid-tones, neutral UI elements
**RGB**: (105, 105, 105)
**Description**: Neutral mid-tone gray.

#### #A9A9A9 - Dark Gray
**Usage**: Light gray UI, disabled states
**RGB**: (169, 169, 169)
**Description**: Light gray for subtle elements.

### Accent Colors (Warm Tones)

#### #B8860B - Dark Goldenrod
**Usage**: Important highlights, warnings, golden accents
**RGB**: (184, 134, 11)
**Description**: Warm gold for emphasis.

#### #FFE4B5 - Moccasin
**Usage**: Very light accents, paper textures
**RGB**: (255, 228, 181)
**Description**: Cream color for light elements.

#### #DEB887 - Burlywood
**Usage**: Skin tones, wood textures
**RGB**: (222, 184, 135)
**Description**: Natural skin tone base.

### Special Colors

#### #CD853F - Peru
**Usage**: Terracotta, clay objects, warm objects
**RGB**: (205, 133, 63)
**Description**: Earthy terracotta tone.

#### #8FBC8F - Dark Sea Green
**Usage**: Vegetation, plants, nature elements
**RGB**: (143, 188, 143)
**Description**: Muted green for subtle contrast.

#### #556B2F - Dark Olive Green
**Usage**: Dark vegetation, military elements
**RGB**: (85, 107, 47)
**Description**: Dark muted green.

#### #B22222 - Firebrick
**Usage**: Danger, warnings, important UI alerts
**RGB**: (178, 34, 34)
**Description**: Muted red for warnings.

#### #4682B4 - Steel Blue
**Usage**: Water, sky, cool accents
**RGB**: (70, 130, 180)
**Description**: Muted blue for cool tones.

#### #000000 - Black
**Usage**: True black for outlines, text
**RGB**: (0, 0, 0)
**Description**: Pure black for strong contrast.

#### #FFFFFF - White (or Transparent)
**Usage**: Highlights, text, transparency
**RGB**: (255, 255, 255)
**Description**: Pure white or reserved for alpha channel.

## Usage Guidelines

### UI Elements

**Buttons**:
- Base: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown)
- Border: #2F4F4F (Dark Slate Gray)
- Text: #FFE4B5 (Moccasin)
- Hover: #D2691E (Chocolate)

**Panels**:
- Background: #D2691E (Chocolate)
- Border: #2F4F4F (Dark Slate Gray)
- Header: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown)

**Text**:
- Primary: #FFE4B5 (Moccasin)
- Secondary: #A9A9A9 (Dark Gray)
- Warning: #B22222 (Firebrick)

### Characters

**Skin Tones**:
- Base: #DEB887 (Burlywood)
- Shadows: #CD853F (Peru)
- Highlights: #FFE4B5 (Moccasin)

**Clothing (1990s Turkey)**:
- Neutral: #696969 (Dim Gray), #A9A9A9 (Dark Gray)
- Warm: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown), #D2691E (Chocolate)
- Accents: #B8860B (Dark Goldenrod)

### Backgrounds

**Urban (Cities)**:
- Buildings: #D2691E, #CD853F, #8B4513
- Sky: #4682B4 (Steel Blue), #A9A9A9 (Dark Gray)
- Roads: #696969 (Dim Gray), #2F4F4F (Dark Slate Gray)

**Nature**:
- Vegetation: #8FBC8F (Dark Sea Green), #556B2F (Dark Olive Green)
- Earth: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown), #CD853F (Peru)

## Do's and Don'ts

### ‚úÖ DO
- Use the 16-color palette exclusively
- Maintain warm, earthy aesthetic
- Use muted, retro tones
- Create depth with limited colors
- Test colors on CRT filter preview

### ‚ùå DON'T
- Add colors outside the palette
- Use neon or saturated colors
- Mix modern color schemes
- Use gradients (pixel art style)
- Ignore the retro aesthetic
